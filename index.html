About MLAAHL

MLA-ACADEMY OF HIGHER LEARNING was established in August 2006 and is located in Bangalore, Karnataka. The MLA Academy of Higher Learning is a premier College in Bangalore known for the highest academic standards, different educational programs, qualified faculty members, and various co-curricular activities and modern infrastructure. MLA Academy of Higher Learning affiliated to Bengaluru City University. The college sustains the highest global standards and best practices in higher education.

At the Academy of Higher Learning, we believe in holistic development of students & hence all our efforts are directed towards building knowledge, skill, social, emotional, and value quotient in our students. We nurture talent & encourage the students to participate in co-curricular & extra-curricular activities.
